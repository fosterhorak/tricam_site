Technology

Working Smarter with Leveraged Fiber Optic

Card 1

The main technical advantage with the Tricam system design is how we leverage our fiber optic infrastructure. By multiplexing every type of signal over fiber optic we are able to set up faster, have more flexibility and strike quickly.

Card 2

This saves you time and money on labor costs, hotel costs, and can save on venue costs. Our fiber topology delivers built in redundancy to every node in our system.

Card 3

The other big advantage is that fiber offers no distance restrictions on cable lengths. Often, we can take a single cable with 12 fiber strands from the truck to the event area. Signals that we transport over fiber include; data, video, audio, and intercom. This single cable, which is armored, can handle every aspect of your show.

Card 4

Less cables going to and from the truck reduces trip hazards and reduces cable clutter. Leveraged Fiber is simply a smarter way to work.
