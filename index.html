Technology

Working Smarter with Leveraged Fiber Optic

Our technical advantage lies in how we leverage our fiber optic infrastructure.

Faster

By multiplexing every type of signal over fiber optic we can set up faster, have more flexibility, and strike quickly.

This saves you time and money on labor, hotel, and even venue costs.

Safer

Fiber optics have no limits on cable length. We can often reach the event area from the trailer with a single cable.

Fewer cables means fewer tripping hazards & less cable clutter.

Smarter

One of our 12-fiber armored cables carries data, video, audio, & intercom at once

Our fiber topology delivers built-in redundancy at every node in the system

A single cable can handle every aspect of your show

Leveraged Fiber is simply a smarter way to work.
